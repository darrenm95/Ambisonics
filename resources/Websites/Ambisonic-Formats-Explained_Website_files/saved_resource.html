        Just can't warm up to ambisonics, no matter how hard I try. The math IS clever and has applications for games and headphone-wearers. But at its core, it's based on the notion of coincident mics--there is none of the critical time information that creates a robust image. Whether stereo, quad, or some higher-order format, ambisonics is based on the notion of a sweet spot. Move out of the sweet spot and the whole thing collapses. If you want a pleasant and convincing experience for everyone in the listening space, you'll use another format.
